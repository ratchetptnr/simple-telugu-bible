Simple Telugu Bible

A Bible app designed for elderly Telugu-speaking users. Simple, accessible, and thoughtful.

Get notified when the app becomes publicly available

Built for Real People

🎤 AI Voice Navigation

Speak in Telugu, English, or mix both. Say "మత్తయి మూడు పన్నెండు" and the app navigates to Matthew 3:12. Powered by Gemini AI.

👴 Elderly-Optimized

Large text (14-28pt), adjustable line spacing, high contrast, and large touch targets. Designed specifically for grandmothers and elderly users.

📖 Auto-Save Position

Pick up exactly where you left off. The app remembers your book, chapter, and verse.

📱 Cross-Platform

Native iOS (SwiftUI) and Android (Jetpack Compose) with identical features and design.

🌐 Offline Support

Full Telugu Bible text works offline. Voice navigation requires internet for AI processing.

🎯 Simple & Focused

No ads, no clutter, no complicated menus. Just the Bible and an easy way to read it.
