Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

Simple Telugu Bible ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Simple Telugu Bible mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Simple Telugu Bible is designed with privacy as a priority. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements

Data Storage

All data used by the app is stored locally on your device:

  • Bible Text: All 66 Bible books are stored within the app and do not require internet access
  • Reading Preferences: Your settings (font size, line spacing, theme) are stored locally on your device
  • Reading Position: Your last read book, chapter, and verse are saved locally to help you continue where you left off

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Permissions

The basic version of Simple Telugu Bible (v1.0) requires zero permissions. The app does not request access to:

  • Internet or network access
  • Camera or microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts or phone information
  • Storage or file access

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are no tracking pixels, cookies, or similar technologies.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your information being intercepted or misused. All app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
